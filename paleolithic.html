PLAY MY GAME:

I made this game over four years ago on Scratch under a different account name and no one has ever played it. You move the two sets of cavemen around with the arrow keys. The up and down arrows move the cavemen up and down the brontosauruses. The left and right arrows make the other caveman pull the rock left and right. Hit the spacebar to launch the rock and hit the UFO. Click here to play: Paleolithic Encounters

It's easy. Just click the green flag to start it. You can click the blue rectangle in the upper left to make it fullscreen if you want but who cares.
