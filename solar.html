BEHOLD THE SOLAR SYTEM:

I drew the solar system in AlphaCAM. Download it below.


I scooted everything together for the screenshot. There's far, far more space between the planets than that. I grabbed the radii and aphelion from wikipedia and drew it to scale at 1/10,000th inch = 1 kilometer. This is fun because learning about the planets in school you never really get a feel for the vast distances between planets and it's hard to illustrate with a picture. But in AlphaCAM we can zoom! So download it below and zoom around in the solar system and see just how much of it is empty space. Be sure to kick it into 3D mode. Hint: if you get lost turn on "show breaks".


Pluto is so far out that AlphaCAM chokes and draws it as a piece of crap:


If you want to have fun try drawing the nearest star to scale and then after that try drawing the nearest galaxy. Good luck.
