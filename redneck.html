REDNECK MACHINING:

Needed a 5/32" groove along the edge. I took a blade from hand fab and put it on a cone. I ran it twice; about 1/16 lower the 2nd run. There's an annoying squiggly black marker line all along the edge. Also note they already tried once by hand for some reason and it was too wavy so they filled it with glue and gave it to me. I was too lazy to research the correct RPM and feedrate. I ended up doing about 5000RPM and only 16"/min. I have a feeling I could have gone a lot faster. What do you think?
