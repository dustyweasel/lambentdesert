Special Help

Look people, if I was an expert computer programmer I wouldn't be working at a granite factory. This website isn't google. You can't enter spaces into the search bar and expect it to work. You can't enter more than one word. Probably you shouldn't be entering anything that isn't a letter or a number. In fact, here's the line that searches for dxf files in lambentdesert.com's stock sink drawings:

find ./sinks/dxfs -type f -iname \*$QUERY_STRING\* -name "*.dxf"

That's it! All it does is see if what you typed is within the name of any of our files. So just enter eluh. Or just enter 3118. Or just enter 035. Just simple fragments of names. Imagine if the sink is named 502.254.98 on lambentdesert.com and you search for 50225498. You won't find it! What if you search for 502-254-98? You won't find it! What if you enter 502 254? You won't find it! Just enter 502. Or just enter 254. That's it! It works! I don't need to go get a Phd. in Computer Science, the search bar works well enough.

The search results will be split into sections. 1) lambentdesert.com stock dxf sinks. Then 2) 3) 4) etc will be results from all the donated folders. Then the last section will be any AlphaCAM sinks that I drew. As you get to trust drawings from certain folders you'll know if you want to double check dimensions or not because you'll always know whose stash you're downloading from.
